Selected Work

A few examples of brands, moments, and challenges I've had the opportunity to shape.

Brand Launch

Genesis Motors U.S. Launch

Introducing a new luxury automotive brand to the American market

When Hyundai Motor Company spun off Genesis as a standalone luxury brand, it needed to enter the U.S. market not just as a new nameplate, but as a credible competitor to BMW, Mercedes, and Lexus. I led experiential and brand activation strategy during this critical launch window — building consumer touchpoints that communicated Genesis's design-forward identity and Korean heritage in a way that felt aspirational rather than derivative.

The work spanned auto show debuts, test-drive programs, dealer launch events, and VIP media moments. Every touchpoint was designed to shape a first impression for a brand with zero U.S. equity — and turn awareness into consideration.

Key Contributions

  • Developed experiential strategy across U.S. auto shows and consumer-facing launch events
  • Created brand education programming for dealer network nationwide
  • Built integrated sponsorship and media activation frameworks to extend launch reach
  • Contributed to early U.S. brand positioning that helped Genesis establish a distinct luxury identity
Brand Development

Tiny Traveler

Building a family travel brand from scratch

Tiny Traveler is a family travel content brand I founded and built independently — creating everything from brand identity and editorial strategy to audience development and monetization. What started as a personal project became a real business: a community of parents who travel with intention, and a platform that brands trusted to reach them.

This project gave me hands-on experience across every dimension of brand building — strategy, content, social, partnerships, and revenue. It also deepened my understanding of what it actually takes to grow an audience from zero and convert it into a sustainable business.

Key Results

  • Built brand identity, voice, and visual system from scratch
  • Grew engaged social community across Instagram and Pinterest
  • Secured paid partnerships with travel and family lifestyle brands
  • Developed content strategy that drove consistent organic reach and engagement
Revenue Growth

FG|PG Pandemic Pivot

Turning crisis into $20M+ in new revenue

When the pandemic hit in 2020, live events — the core revenue driver for FG|PG — stopped overnight. As a senior leader, I helped architect the agency's pivot: identifying adjacent opportunities, repositioning our capabilities, and moving fast to replace lost revenue with new business in digital, virtual, and content-driven activations.

The result wasn't just survival — it was growth. We emerged from the pandemic with a more diversified service offering, new client relationships, and a stronger case for the agency's strategic value beyond event production.

Key Results

  • Led strategic response that generated $20M+ in net-new revenue during pandemic years
  • Developed and sold virtual and hybrid event capabilities to existing and new clients
  • Expanded agency positioning into digital content and brand strategy services
  • Retained key client relationships through crisis by delivering high-value alternative programs
Global Programs

Hyundai Global Experiential Program

Scaling brand experiences across international markets

Over several years of work with Hyundai Motor Company, I helped develop and scale experiential programming across global markets — from major auto shows in North America and Europe to brand activations tied to high-profile sponsorships including FIFA and the Olympics. The work required coordinating across international teams, managing complex logistics, and ensuring brand consistency across vastly different cultural and competitive contexts.

At its best, this work helped Hyundai show up as more than a brand with a point of view on mobility, design, and the future. My role was to bring that to life in physical and live environments in a way that was locally relevant and globally coherent.

Key Results

  • Managed experiential activations across North America, Europe, and international auto shows
  • Developed integrated brand experiences tied to FIFA World Cup and Olympic sponsorships
  • Built scalable frameworks for global event execution with regional customization
  • Contributed to brand perception shifts tracked through post-event research and media metrics
